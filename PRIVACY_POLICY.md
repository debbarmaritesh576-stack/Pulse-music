# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026

Pulse Music is a **100% offline** music player. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What We DON'T Do
- ❌ No internet access required
- ❌ No data collection
- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No tracking
- ❌ No ads
- ❌ No account needed

## What We Access
- 📁 **Device Storage** — Only to read your music files
- 🎵 **MediaStore** — Android's built-in music database
